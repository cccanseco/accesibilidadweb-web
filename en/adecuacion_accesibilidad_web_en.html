Adaptation and Development of Accesible Websites

Web Accessibility level audit of a site. We carry out a study complete with recommendations for the creation of a new site or for the modification of the existing one, as well as the timetable necessary to achieve the desired level of compliance (A, AA, AAA). We base our web Accessibility on the guidelines recommended by the WAI / W3C as well as Spanish code UNE 139803.

Because of the large quantity of information normally found on a website, we use a content management system.

For Goverment Institutions, we apply the double A (AA) level of Accessibility in compliance with the appropriate legislation (LSSICE) which deals with generally recognized guidelines concerning accessibility. The guidelines that we use are described in Resolución del Parlamento Europeo sobre la Comunicación de la Comisión "eEurope 2002: Accesibilidad de los sitios Web públicos y de su contenido" (COM(2001) 529 - C5-0074/2002-2002/2032(COS)) published by the official publication of the European Union dated October 30, 2003. Point 31 of the resolution states that for websites to be considered accessible, they must comply with the double A (AA) level and the second priority of the WAI guidelines must be met.
